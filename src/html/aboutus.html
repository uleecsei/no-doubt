First steps in music

Stanislav Lysenko (born May 15, 1980) is an American musician, record producer, songwriter, and animal rights activist. Stanislav is the bassist and co-writer for the American rock bands No Doubt and Dreamcar. He was born in Soviet Union and moved to America in 1984. His music career started with the first present from his nanny. It was a drum. Starting this moment his musical work outside of the band includes producing and songwriting for artists such as Pink, Weezer, Elan Atias and No Doubt bandmate Gwen Stefani, among others.
